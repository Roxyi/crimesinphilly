Crimes in Philly

A project by

Yi Xu

This is a web mapping interface displaying the crimes in Philly during the time period from 2015-01-01 to 2016-04-12.
It aims to help the citizens in Philadelphia know more about crimes in the neighborhood and avoid the dangerous corners.
Data Source: Year-to-Date Part I Crime Incidents
